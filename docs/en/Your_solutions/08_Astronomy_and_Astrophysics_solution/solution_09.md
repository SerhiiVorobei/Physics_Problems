# Solution 09 — Aristarchus method

## Given

θ = 89.85°  
α = 0.53°  
d_EM = 3.84×10⁵ km

---

## Earth–Sun distance

tan(θ) = d_ES / d_EM

θ ≈ 89.85° → very large ratio

d_ES ≈ d_EM / cos(0.15°)

d_ES ≈ 1.5×10⁸ km

---

## Sun diameter

α = 0.53° = 0.00925 rad

D_S = α × d_ES

D_S ≈ 1.39×10⁶ km

---

## Ratio

D_M / D_S ≈ 0.27

---

## Sensitivity

If θ = 89.75° → noticeable change (~% level)

Small angle error → large distance error

---

## Final insight

Aristarchus method is extremely sensitive to angular precision.
