# Solution 08 — Interplanetary travel

Distance = 55 million km

---

## a) Light speed

t = 55×10⁶ / 300,000

t ≈ 183 s ≈ 3.05 min

---

## b) Spacecraft (11.11 km/s)

t = 55×10⁶ / (11.11×3600)

t ≈ 1375 h ≈ 57 days

---

## c) Airplane (900 km/h)

t = 55×10⁶ / 900

t ≈ 61,111 h ≈ 6.97 years

---

## Final answers

a) ~3 min  
b) ~57 days  
c) ~7 years
