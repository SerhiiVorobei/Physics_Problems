# Solution 02 — Orbital mechanics

## ISS orbit

R = 6378 + 400 = 6778 km

v = √(GM/R)

v ≈ √(3.986×10¹⁴ / 6.778×10⁶)

v ≈ 7.67 km/s

---

## Earth orbit around Sun

v = 2πR / T

R = 1.5×10⁸ km  
T = 365.25 days

v ≈ 29.8 km/s

---

## Comparison

Earth around Sun is faster

---

## Final answer

ISS: 7.7 km/s  
Earth orbit: 29.8 km/s → faster
