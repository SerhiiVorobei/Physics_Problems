# Solution 05 — Escape velocity Moon

## Formula

v = √(2GM/R)

---

## Moon values

M = 7.35×10²² kg  
R = 1.737×10⁶ m

---

## Result

v ≈ 2.38 km/s

---

## Comparison

Earth escape velocity = 11.2 km/s

Ratio ≈ 0.21

---

## Final answer

v ≈ 2.38 km/s ≈ 0.21 of Earth
