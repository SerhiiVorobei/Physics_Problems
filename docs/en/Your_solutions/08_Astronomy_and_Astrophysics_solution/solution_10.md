# Solution 10 — Height of atmosphere

## 1. Convert time to angle

t = 40 min = 2/3 hour

Earth rotates 360° in 24 h

ϕ = 360 × (2/3) / 24

ϕ = 10°

---

## 2. Atmosphere model

cosϕ = RE / (RE + h)

---

## 3. Solve

RE = 6370 km  
cos10° ≈ 0.985

RE / cosϕ ≈ 6475 km

h ≈ 105 km

---

## Final answer

ϕ = 10°  
h ≈ 105 km
