# Solution 04 — Force Comparison

## 1. Coulomb force

F_e = k e² / r²

F_e ≈ 8.99×10⁹ × (1.6×10⁻¹⁹)² / (5.3×10⁻¹¹)²

F_e ≈ 8.2×10⁻⁸ N

---

## 2. Gravitational force

F_g = G m_p m_e / r²

F_g ≈ 6.67×10⁻¹¹ × (1.67×10⁻²⁷)(9.11×10⁻³¹) / (5.3×10⁻¹¹)²

F_g ≈ 3.6×10⁻⁴⁷ N

---

## 3. Ratio

F_e / F_g ≈ 2×10³⁹

---

## Final answer

Electric force is ~10³⁹ times stronger than gravity.
