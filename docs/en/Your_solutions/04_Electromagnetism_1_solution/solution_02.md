# Solution 02 — Electric Potential

## 1. Key idea

Electric potential is scalar → no direction, only sum.

---

## 2. Formula

V = k Σ(q / r)

All charges are at equal distance from center:

r = a/√2 = 1/√2

---

## 3. Sum of charges

V ∝ (1 − 2 + 3 − 4) = −2

---

## 4. Result

V = k * (−2) / (1/√2)

V = −2k√2

---

## Final answer

V ≈ −2√2 k
