# Solution 03 — Electrostatic Equilibrium

## 1. Setup

q1 = +4 C  
q2 = +9 C  
distance = 2 m

Let x be distance from q1.

---

## 2. Condition

For equilibrium:

k·4/x² = k·9/(2 − x)²

---

## 3. Solve

4(2 − x)² = 9x²

2(2 − x) = 3x

4 − 2x = 3x

x = 0.8 m

---

## Final answer

q3 is placed at 0.8 m from q1
