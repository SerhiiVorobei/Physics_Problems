# Solution 07 — Cyclotron Motion

## 1. Energy gain

eV = (1/2)mv²

v = √(2eV/m)

v ≈ √(2×1.6×10⁻¹⁹×5000 / 9.11×10⁻³¹)

v ≈ 4.2×10⁷ m/s

---

## 2. Radius in magnetic field

r = mv / (eB)

r = (9.11×10⁻³¹ × 4.2×10⁷) / (1.6×10⁻¹⁹ × 0.1)

r ≈ 2.4×10⁻³ m

---

## Final answer

r ≈ 2.4 mm
