# Solution 01 — Coulomb’s Law

## 1. Symmetry

Four +1 C charges are placed symmetrically at the corners of a square.

The -2 C charge is at the center.

---

## 2. Key idea

All four electric forces have:
- equal magnitude
- symmetric directions

Opposite charges attract equally from all four corners.

---

## 3. Result

Horizontal components cancel:
- left/right cancel
- up/down cancel

---

## Final answer

F_net = 0 N

---

## Interpretation

Even though each force is nonzero, perfect symmetry leads to full cancellation.
