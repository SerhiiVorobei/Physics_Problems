# Solution 10 — Speed of light measurement

## Given

f = 2.45 GHz = 2.45×10⁹ Hz

---

## Formula

c = λ f

---

## Result

c_measured = λ × 2.45×10⁹

---

## Comparison

c_true = 3.0×10⁸ m/s

---

## Error

error% = |c_measured − c_true| / c_true × 100%

---

## Final note

Result depends on measured wavelength accuracy from chocolate pattern.
