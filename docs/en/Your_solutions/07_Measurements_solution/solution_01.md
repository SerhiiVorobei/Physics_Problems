# Solution 01 — Volume of sphere with uncertainty

## Given
r = (6.20 ± 0.05) cm

---

## Volume formula

V = (4/3)πr³

---

## Central value

V = (4/3)π(6.20)³  
V ≈ 997.6 cm³

---

## Uncertainty propagation

ΔV/V = 3(Δr/r)

ΔV/V = 3(0.05/6.20)

ΔV/V ≈ 0.0242

---

## Absolute uncertainty

ΔV ≈ 0.0242 × 997.6 ≈ 24.1 cm³

---

## Final answer

V = (998 ± 24) cm³
