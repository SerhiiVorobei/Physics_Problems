# Solution 02 — Area with uncertainty

## Given

L = (15.3 ± 0.1) cm  
W = (8.4 ± 0.1) cm

---

## Area

A = L × W = 128.52 cm²

---

## Relative uncertainty

ΔA/A = ΔL/L + ΔW/W

= 0.1/15.3 + 0.1/8.4  
≈ 0.00654 + 0.0119 = 0.0184

---

## Absolute uncertainty

ΔA ≈ 0.0184 × 128.52 ≈ 2.37 cm²

---

## Final answer

A = (128.5 ± 2.4) cm²
