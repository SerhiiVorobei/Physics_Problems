# Solution 08 — Mass-spring experiment (HTML concept)

## Procedure

1. Simulate harmonic motion
2. Measure time for 10 oscillations (10 trials)
3. Compute:
   - mean period T
   - standard deviation σ

---

## Spring constant

T = 2π√(m/k)

k = 4π²m / T²

---

## Uncertainty

Δk/k = 2(ΔT/T)

---

## Output

- T_mean from data
- σ from statistics
- k ± Δk
