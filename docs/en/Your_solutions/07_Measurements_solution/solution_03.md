# Solution 03 — Resistance with uncertainty

## Given

V = (10.0 ± 0.2) V  
I = (2.00 ± 0.05) A

---

## Value

R = V / I = 5 Ω

---

## Relative uncertainty

ΔR/R = ΔV/V + ΔI/I

= 0.2/10 + 0.05/2  
= 0.02 + 0.025 = 0.045

---

## Absolute uncertainty

ΔR = 0.045 × 5 = 0.225 Ω

---

## Final answer

R = (5.00 ± 0.23) Ω
