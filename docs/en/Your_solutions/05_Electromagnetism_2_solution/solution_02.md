# Solution 02 — Ampere's Law

## 1. Setup

Two wires:
- distance d = 0.1 m
- midpoint r = 0.05 m
- currents: 5 A opposite directions

---

## 2. Magnetic field from one wire

B = μ₀ I / (2πr)

---

## 3. Each wire

B₁ = (4π×10⁻⁷ × 5) / (2π × 0.05)
B₁ = 2×10⁻⁵ T

---

## 4. Direction

At midpoint:
- fields add (same direction due to opposite currents)

---

## 5. Total field

B_total = 2B₁ = 4×10⁻⁵ T

---

## Final answer

B = 4×10⁻⁵ T (perpendicular to plane of wires)
