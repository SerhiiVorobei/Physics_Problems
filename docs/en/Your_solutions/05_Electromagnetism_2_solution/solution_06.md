# Solution 06 — EM Wave Analysis

## 1. Given

E_y = 100 sin(10⁷ x − ωt)

---

## 2. Direction

Form sin(kx − ωt) → propagation in +x direction

---

## 3. Wave number

k = 10⁷

λ = 2π / k

λ ≈ 6.28×10⁻⁷ m

---

## 4. Angular frequency

ω remains symbolic (not given numerically)

---

## 5. Magnetic field

B = E / c

B_y = (100 / 3×10⁸) sin(10⁷ x − ωt)

---

## Final answers

- direction: +x  
- λ ≈ 6.3×10⁻⁷ m  
- B amplitude ≈ 3.3×10⁻⁷ T
