# Solution 05 — Capacitor

## 1. Given

S = 0.02 m²  
d = 5 mm = 5×10⁻³ m  
V = 500 V  

---

## 2. Capacitance

C = ε₀ S / d

C = (8.85×10⁻¹² × 0.02) / 5×10⁻³

C = 3.54×10⁻¹¹ F

---

## 3. Energy

U = (1/2) C V²

U = 0.5 × 3.54×10⁻¹¹ × 2.5×10⁵

U ≈ 4.4×10⁻⁶ J

---

## 4. Electric field

E = V / d

E = 500 / 5×10⁻³ = 1×10⁵ V/m

---

## 5. Force

F = (1/2) ε₀ S E²

F ≈ 1.77×10⁻² N

---

## Final answers

C ≈ 3.5×10⁻¹¹ F  
U ≈ 4.4×10⁻⁶ J  
E ≈ 1×10⁵ V/m  
F ≈ 1.8×10⁻² N
