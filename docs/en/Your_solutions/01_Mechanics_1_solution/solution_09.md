# Solution 09 — Momentum Comparison

## 1. Given data

Fly:
- mass m₁ = 2 g = 0.002 kg
- velocity v₁ = 10 m/s

Tennis ball:
- mass m₂ = 60 g = 0.06 kg
- velocity v₂ = 1 m/s

---

## 2. Momentum formula

p = mv

---

## 3. Fly momentum

p₁ = 0.002 × 10 = 0.02 kg·m/s

---

## 4. Tennis ball momentum

p₂ = 0.06 × 1 = 0.06 kg·m/s

---

## 5. Comparison

p₂ > p₁

So the tennis ball has greater momentum.

---

## 6. Interpretation

Even though the fly is faster, the much larger mass of the tennis ball results in higher momentum.
Momentum depends linearly on mass and velocity.
