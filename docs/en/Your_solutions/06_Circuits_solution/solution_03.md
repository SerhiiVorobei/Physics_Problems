## Section 3 — Mixed Circuit  
**Given:** All resistors are 5 Ω  
**Task:** Find the equivalent resistance of the circuit.

### Step 1 — Identify series and parallel groups
- The two vertical resistors on the left form a **series pair**:  
  R_left = 5 + 5 = 10 Ω

- The two vertical resistors on the right also form a **series pair**:  
  R_right = 5 + 5 = 10 Ω

- The two horizontal resistors in the middle form a **parallel pair**:  
  1 / R_mid = 1/5 + 1/5 = 2/5  
  R_mid = 5/2 = 2.5 Ω

### Step 2 — Combine the three main blocks
Now the circuit becomes three resistances in **parallel**:

- Left block: 10 Ω  
- Middle block: 2.5 Ω  
- Right block: 10 Ω  

So:

1 / R_eq = 1/10 + 1/2.5 + 1/10  
1 / R_eq = 0.1 + 0.4 + 0.1 = 0.6  

R_eq = 1 / 0.6 = 1.666… Ω

### Final Answer
**R_eq ≈ 1.67 Ω**
