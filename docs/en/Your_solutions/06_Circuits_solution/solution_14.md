# Section 14 — RLC Circuit Equation

## Differential equation (series RLC)

L d²Q/dt² + R dQ/dt + (1/C) Q = V(t)

---

## Current form

Since I = dQ/dt:

L dI/dt + R I + (1/C) ∫ I dt = V(t)

---

## Analogy to damped harmonic oscillator

Mechanical form:
m x'' + b x' + k x = F(t)

---

## Correspondence

- L ↔ mass m (inertia)
- R ↔ damping coefficient b
- 1/C ↔ spring constant k
- Q ↔ displacement x
- I ↔ velocity x'
- V(t) ↔ external force F(t)
