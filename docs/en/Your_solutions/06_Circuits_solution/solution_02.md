# Section 2 — Resistors (1 Ω each)

## Task

Find all possible equivalent resistances using three identical 1 Ω resistors.

---

## Configurations

### All series
R = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3 Ω  

---

### All parallel
R = 1/3 Ω  

---

### Two in series, parallel with third
R = 2/3 Ω  

---

### Two in parallel, series with third
R = 3/2 Ω  

---

## Final Answer

1/3 Ω, 2/3 Ω, 3/2 Ω, 3 Ω
