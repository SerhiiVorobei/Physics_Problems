# Section 1 — Series and Parallel Circuit

## Given

R1 = 15 Ω  
R2 = 30 Ω  
R3 = 50 Ω  
V = 12 V  

---

## Series connection

R_series = R1 + R2 + R3  
R_series = 15 + 30 + 50 = 95 Ω  

I = V / R_series  
I = 12 / 95 ≈ 0.126 A  

---

## Parallel connection

1 / R_parallel = 1/15 + 1/30 + 1/50  

1/15 = 10/150  
1/30 = 5/150  
1/50 = 3/150  

1 / R_parallel = 18/150 = 3/25  

R_parallel = 25/3 ≈ 8.33 Ω  

I = V / R_parallel  
I = 12 / (25/3) = 36/25 = 1.44 A
