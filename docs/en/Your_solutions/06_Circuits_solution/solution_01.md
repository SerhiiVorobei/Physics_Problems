Solution 01 — Series and Parallel Circuit

1. Given  
   - \(R_1 = 15\,\Omega\)  
   - \(R_2 = 30\,\Omega\)  
   - \(R_3 = 50\,\Omega\)  
   - \(V = 12\,\text{V}\)

2. Series connection  
   Total resistance:
   

\[
   R_{\text{eq,series}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3 = 15 + 30 + 50 = 95\,\Omega
   \]


   Current from the battery:
   

\[
   I_{\text{series}} = \frac{V}{R_{\text{eq,series}}}
   = \frac{12}{95}\,\text{A} \approx 0.126\,\text{A}
   \]



3. Parallel connection  
   Total resistance:
   

\[
   \frac{1}{R_{\text{eq,parallel}}}
   = \frac{1}{15} + \frac{1}{30} + \frac{1}{50}
   = \frac{10}{150} + \frac{5}{150} + \frac{3}{150}
   = \frac{18}{150} = \frac{3}{25}
   \]


   

\[
   R_{\text{eq,parallel}} = \frac{25}{3}\,\Omega \approx 8.33\,\Omega
   \]


   Current from the battery:
   

\[
   I_{\text{parallel}} = \frac{V}{R_{\text{eq,parallel}}}
   = \frac{12}{25/3} = 12 \cdot \frac{3}{25}
   = \frac{36}{25}\,\text{A} = 1.44\,\text{A}
   \]



4. Final answers  
   - Series: \(R_{\text{eq}} = 95\,\Omega,\; I \approx 0.126\,\text{A}\)  
   - Parallel: \(R_{\text{eq}} = \dfrac{25}{3}\,\Omega \approx 8.33\,\Omega,\; I = 1.44\,\text{A}\)
