# Section 7 — Capacitors in Parallel

## Given
C1 = 4 μF  
C2 = 6 μF  
V = 10 V  

---

## Total capacitance (parallel)

C_total = C1 + C2  
C_total = 4 + 6 = 10 μF  

---

## Total charge stored

Q = C_total · V  
Q = 10 μF × 10 V = 100 μC  

---

## Total energy stored

U = (1/2) C V²  
U = (1/2) × 10×10⁻⁶ × 10²  
U = 0.5 × 10×10⁻⁶ × 100  
U = 5×10⁻⁴ J
