# Solution 01 — de Broglie wavelength

## Electron (150 eV)

λ = h / p  
p = √(2mE)

λ ≈ 1.0×10⁻¹⁰ m = 0.10 nm

---

## Golf ball

m = 0.05 kg, v = 60 m/s

λ = h / mv

λ ≈ 2.2×10⁻³⁴ m

---

## Conclusion

Quantum effects are negligible for macroscopic objects because λ is extremely small.
