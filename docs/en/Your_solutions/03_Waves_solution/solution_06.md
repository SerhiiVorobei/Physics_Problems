# Solution 06 — Wave Equation

## Given

y(x,t) = 0.05 sin(2πx − 50πt)

---

## 1. Amplitude

A = 0.05 m

---

## 2. Wave number

k = 2π → λ = 1 m

---

## 3. Angular frequency

ω = 50π

f = ω / (2π) = 25 Hz

---

## 4. Wave speed

v = fλ = 25 × 1 = 25 m/s

---

## Final answers

- A = 0.05 m  
- λ = 1 m  
- f = 25 Hz  
- v = 25 m/s
