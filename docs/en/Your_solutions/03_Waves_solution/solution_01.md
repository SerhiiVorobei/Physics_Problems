# Solution 01 — Wave Properties

## 1. Given

f = 440 Hz

Speed in air: v₁ = 343 m/s  
Speed in water: v₂ = 1482 m/s

---

## 2. Wavelength in air

λ = v / f

λ_air = 343 / 440 ≈ 0.78 m

---

## 3. Wavelength in water

λ_water = 1482 / 440 ≈ 3.37 m

---

## Final answers

- λ_air ≈ 0.78 m  
- λ_water ≈ 3.37 m
