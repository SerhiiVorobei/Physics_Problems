# Solution 02 — Harmonic Motion

## 1. Given

x(t) = 0.2 cos(10πt)  
m = 10 kg

Angular frequency:

ω = 10π

---

## 2. Spring constant

ω² = k/m

k = mω²

k = 10 × (10π)²

k = 1000π² ≈ 9869 N/m

---

## 3. Total energy

E = (1/2)kA²

A = 0.2 m

E = 0.5 × 9869 × 0.04

E ≈ 197.4 J

---

## Final answers

- k ≈ 9869 N/m  
- E ≈ 197 J
