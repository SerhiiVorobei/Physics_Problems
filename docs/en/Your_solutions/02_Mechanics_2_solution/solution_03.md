# Solution 03 — Conservation of Energy

## 1. Given

L = 1 m  
θ = 15°

---

## 2. Height difference

h = L(1 − cosθ)

h ≈ 1(1 − 0.966)

h ≈ 0.034 m

---

## 3. Energy conservation

mgh = (1/2)mv²

v = √(2gh)

v = √(2 × 9.81 × 0.034)

v ≈ 0.82 m/s

---

## Final answer

v ≈ 0.82 m/s
