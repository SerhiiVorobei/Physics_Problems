# Solution 08 — Variable Force F(x) = −kx

## 1. Equation of motion

F = m x''

So:

m x'' = −kx

x'' + (k/m)x = 0

This is harmonic motion.

---

## 2. Solution

x(t) = A cos(ωt) + B sin(ωt)

where:

ω = √(k/m)

---

## 3. Work done

W = ∫ F dx = ∫ (−kx) dx

W = −(1/2)kx² | from 0 to x₀

W = −(1/2)k x₀²

---

## 4. Potential energy

U(x) = (1/2)kx²

So:

F = −dU/dx

---

## 5. Interpretation

- Force is restoring
- Energy oscillates between kinetic and potential
