# Solution 12 — Constant force motion

## 1. Given

F = (6, 2)  
m = 2 kg

---

## 2. Acceleration

a = F/m = (3, 1)

---

## 3. Velocity

v(t) = (3t + 1, t − 1)

---

## 4. Position

r(t) = (1.5t² + t, 0.5t² − t)

---

## 5. Trajectory

Eliminate t:

t = y + 1

x = 1.5(y + 1)² + (y + 1)

→ parabola

---

## 6. Work

W = F · r(3)

r(3) = (15, 3)

W = (6,2)·(15,3) = 96 J

---

## 7. Work-energy theorem

W = ΔK → consistent
