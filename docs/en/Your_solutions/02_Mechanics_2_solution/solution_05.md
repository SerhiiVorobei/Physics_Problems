# Solution 05 — Inelastic Collision

## 1. Given

m₁ = 70 kg  
v₁ = 3 m/s  
m₂ = 140 kg  
v₂ = 0

---

## 2. Momentum conservation

v = (m₁v₁ + m₂v₂) / (m₁ + m₂)

v = (70 × 3) / 210

v = 1 m/s

---

## 3. Energy check

Kinetic energy is NOT conserved in inelastic collision.

Energy is transformed into heat and deformation.

---

## Final answer

v = 1 m/s
