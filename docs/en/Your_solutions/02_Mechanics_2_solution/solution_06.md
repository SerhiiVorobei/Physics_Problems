# Solution 06 — Energy Dissipation

## 1. Given

Initial height: h₀ = 2 m  
Energy loss per bounce: 30%

So remaining energy factor:

0.7 per bounce

---

## 2. After first bounce

h₁ = 0.7 × 2 = 1.4 m

---

## 3. After second bounce

h₂ = 0.7 × 1.4

h₂ = 0.98 m

---

## Final answer

h₂ ≈ 0.98 m
