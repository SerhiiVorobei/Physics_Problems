# Solution 04 — Energy & Momentum

## 1. Speed at bottom

mgh = (1/2)mv²

v = √(2gh)

v = √(2 × 9.81 × 3)

v ≈ 7.67 m/s

---

## 2. Inelastic collision

Momentum conserved:

m₁v₁ = (m₁ + m₂)v

0.5 × 7.67 = 2.0 × v

v ≈ 1.92 m/s

---

## Final answer

v ≈ 1.92 m/s after collision
