# Solution 10 — Force field and power

## 1. Position

x = 5t² − t  
y = 2t³  
z = −3t + 2

---

## 2. Velocity

v = dr/dt

v = (10t − 1, 6t², −3)

---

## 3. Acceleration

a = dv/dt

a = (10, 12t, 0)

---

## 4. Momentum

p = m v, m = 0.5

p = (0.5(10t − 1), 3t², −1.5)

---

## 5. Force

F = m a

F = (5, 6t, 0)

---

## 6. Power

P = F · v

P = (5, 6t, 0) · (10t − 1, 6t², −3)

P = 5(10t − 1) + 36t³

P = 50t − 5 + 36t³
