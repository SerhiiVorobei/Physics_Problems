# Solution 07 — Dynamics with Friction

## 1. Given

m₁ = 10 kg (lower block)  
m₂ = 5 kg (upper block, tied to wall)  
F = 45 N  
μ = 0.2  

Upper block is fixed → only lower block moves.

---

## 2. Forces on 10 kg block

Normal force from ground:

N_ground = (m₁ + m₂)g = 15 × 9.81 = 147.15 N

Friction from ground:

F_f = μ N = 0.2 × 147.15 ≈ 29.43 N

---

## 3. Net force

F_net = 45 − 29.43 = 15.57 N

---

## 4. Acceleration

a = F_net / m₁

a = 15.57 / 10 ≈ 1.56 m/s²

---

## Final answer

a ≈ 1.56 m/s²
