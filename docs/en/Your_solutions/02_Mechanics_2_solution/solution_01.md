# Solution 01 — Gravitational Dependence

## 1. Pendulum period on Moon

Formula:

T = 2π √(L / g)

Since T ∝ 1/√g:

T_Moon = T_Earth √(g_Earth / g_Moon)

Given:

g_Moon = g_Earth / 6

So:

T_Moon = 4 √6 ≈ 9.8 s

---

## 2. Pendulum length for T = 1 s

T = 2π √(L/g)

Solve for L:

L = (g T²) / (4π²)

L = (9.81 × 1²) / (4π²)

L ≈ 0.248 m

---

## Final answers

- Moon period: ≈ 9.8 s  
- Length for 1 s: ≈ 0.25 m
