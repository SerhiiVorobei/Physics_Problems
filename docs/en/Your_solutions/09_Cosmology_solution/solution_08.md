# Solution 08 — Cosmic expansion

## Given

d = 100 million ly  
t = 1 billion years  
H₀ = 70 km/s/Mpc

---

## Convert distance

100 million ly ≈ 30.7 Mpc

---

## Recession speed

v = H₀ d = 70 × 30.7 ≈ 2149 km/s

---

## Time

t = 1 billion years ≈ 3.15×10¹⁶ s

---

## Expansion increase

Δd ≈ v t

Δd ≈ 2149 × 3.15×10¹⁶ km

Δd ≈ 6.8×10¹⁹ km

---

## Convert to ly

≈ 7.2 million ly

---

## Final answer

Increase ≈ 7 million light-years
