# Solution 02 — Cosmic Temperatures

## Order (hot → cold)

1. Universe at nucleosynthesis (~10⁹ K)  
2. Core of the Sun (~1.5×10⁷ K)  
3. Ionization of hydrogen (~10⁴ K)  
4. Surface of the Sun (~5800 K)  
5. Human body (~310 K)  
6. Interstellar space (~10–100 K)  
7. CMB today (2.7 K)  
8. Black hole (10 solar masses) (~10⁻⁸ K)  
9. Universe at recombination (~3000 K)

---

## Final note

Recombination is colder than solar surface but warmer than CMB today.
