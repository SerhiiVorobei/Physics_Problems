# Solution 09 — Doppler effect

## Given

v = 5×10⁶ m/s  
c = 3×10⁸ m/s  
λ = 486 nm

---

## 1. Non-relativistic

λ' = λ (1 + v/c)

λ' ≈ 486 × (1 + 0.0167)

λ' ≈ 494 nm

---

## 2. Relativistic

λ' = λ √((1+β)/(1−β))

β = 0.0167

λ' ≈ 486 × 1.017

λ' ≈ 494 nm

---

## Comparison

Difference is very small (~0.1%)

---

## Final answer

≈ 494 nm (both methods nearly identical)
