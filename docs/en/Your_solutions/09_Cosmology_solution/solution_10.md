# Solution 10 — CMB peak wavelength

## Wien’s law

λ_peak = b / T

b = 2.898×10⁻³ m·K

---

## At recombination

T = 3000 K

λ = 9.66×10⁻⁷ m ≈ 966 nm (infrared)

---

## Today

T = 2.7 K

λ = 1.07×10⁻³ m ≈ 1.07 mm

---

## Final answer

Recombination: ~966 nm  
Today: ~1.1 mm
