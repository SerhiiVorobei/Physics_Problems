# Solution 06 — Galactic collision

## Given

d = 2.5 million ly  
v = 110 km/s

---

## Convert distance

d ≈ 2.36×10¹⁹ m  
v = 1.1×10⁵ m/s

---

## Time

t = d / v

t ≈ 2.15×10¹⁴ s

---

## Convert to years

t ≈ 6.8 million years

---

## Final answer

≈ 7 million years
