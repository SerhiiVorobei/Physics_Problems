# Solution 09 — Twin paradox (ISS)

## Given

v = 7.66 km/s  
t = 1 year

---

## Lorentz factor

v/c ≈ 2.55×10⁻⁵

γ ≈ 1 + 3.25×10⁻¹⁰

---

## Time difference

Δt ≈ 1 year × 3.25×10⁻¹⁰

≈ 10 milliseconds

---

## Final answer

Astronaut is ~0.01 seconds younger after 1 year
