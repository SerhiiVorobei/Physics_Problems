# Solution 07 — Velocity transformation

## Given

v = 0.8c  
u' = 0.5c

---

## Classical

u = 1.3c (impossible physically)

---

## Relativistic

u = (v + u') / (1 + vu'/c²)

u = (0.8 + 0.5) / (1 + 0.4)

u = 1.3 / 1.4

u ≈ 0.929c

---

## Final answer

Classical: 1.3c (invalid)  
Relativistic: 0.93c
