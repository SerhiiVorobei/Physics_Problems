# Solution 10 — Gravitational time dilation

## Given

h = 400 km  
R ≈ 6370 km

---

## Approximation

Effect is very small:

Δt/t ≈ gh/c²

---

## Calculation

≈ (9.8 × 4×10⁵) / (9×10¹⁶)

≈ 4×10⁻¹¹

---

## Over 1 year

≈ 1 second scale difference

---

## Final answer

Astronaut is ~1 second younger per year (order of magnitude)
