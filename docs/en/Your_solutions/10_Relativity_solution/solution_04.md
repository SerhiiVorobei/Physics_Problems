# Solution 04 — Relativistic energy (proton)

## Given

m₀c² = 938 MeV  
v = 0.99c

---

## Lorentz factor

γ ≈ 7.09

---

## Total energy

E = γ m₀c²

E ≈ 7.09 × 938 MeV

E ≈ 6650 MeV

---

## Kinetic energy

K = E − m₀c²

K ≈ 6650 − 938

K ≈ 5712 MeV ≈ 5.71 GeV

---

## Final answer

K ≈ 5.7 GeV
