# Solution 02 — Time dilation (episodes problem)

## Given

Earth: 2 hours  
Ship: 1 hour

---

## Time dilation

γ = t / t₀ = 2

---

## Solve

γ = 1 / √(1 − v²/c²)

2 = 1 / √(1 − v²/c²)

---

## Result

1 − v²/c² = 1/4

v = √(3/4)c

---

## Final answer

v ≈ 0.866c
